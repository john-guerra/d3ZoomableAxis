Penguins scatterplot — zoom via two d3-zoomable-axis (bottom + left)

Drag a half-knob to resize an end, drag the interior band to pan, or drag/double-click a value badge. The scatter zooms live to the selected window. Each axis carries a scented marginal distribution that grows away from the plot (x-histogram downward, y-violin leftward), so it reads like a classic marginal plot instead of covering the points. Click the ⚙ on either axis to retune its scent.
